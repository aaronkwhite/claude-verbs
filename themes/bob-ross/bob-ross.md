# Bob Ross Verb Theme

> "We don't make mistakes, just happy little accidents."

A calm, wholesome verb theme inspired by Bob Ross and *The Joy of Painting*. These verbs capture the gentle, encouraging spirit of Bob's approach to art and life, perfect for bringing a little tranquility to your Claude Code experience.

## Verb List

| Verb | Reference |
|------|-----------|
| Beating the Devil Out of It | Bob's iconic brush cleaning technique |
| Adding Happy Little Trees | His most famous signature phrase |
| Making Happy Accidents | No mistakes, only happy accidents |
| Finding Friends for Trees | "Trees need friends too" |
| Loading Up the Brush | Preparing the brush with paint |
| Mixing Midnight Black | One of his signature colors |
| Pulling Down Some Color | Brush technique for painting |
| Making Big Decisions | "Big decisions" before adding elements |
| Tapping In Some Highlights | Adding light touches to paintings |
| Getting a Little Crazy | When Bob would go wild with technique |
| Believing in the Magic | His belief anyone can paint |

| Creating a Happy World | Painting your own perfect world |
| Whispering to the Canvas | His soft-spoken painting approach |
| Finding Your Bravery | Encouraging viewers to try |
| Making Friends with Nature | His love of natural subjects |

| Scrubbing In Some Color | Base layer painting technique |
