# Lord of the Rings Theme

> *"Even the smallest person can change the course of the future."*
> -- Galadriel

A complete collection of 40 spinner verbs inspired by Peter Jackson's epic Lord of the Rings trilogy. These verbs transform iconic moments, quotes, and scenes from all three films into action-oriented present participles perfect for Claude Code spinners.
