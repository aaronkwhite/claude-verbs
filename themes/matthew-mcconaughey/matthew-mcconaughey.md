# Matthew McConaughey Verb Theme

> "Alright, alright, alright."

A laid-back, philosophical verb theme inspired by Matthew McConaughey's iconic roles and his distinctively Texan approach to life. From the stoner wisdom of *Dazed and Confused* to the existential depths of *True Detective* and *Interstellar*, plus those unforgettable Lincoln commercials, these verbs capture the spirit of a man who turned "just keep livin'" into a lifestyle.
