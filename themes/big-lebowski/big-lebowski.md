# The Big Lebowski Spinner Verbs

> "The Dude abides."

A collection of 40 spinner verbs inspired by the Coen Brothers' 1998 cult classic *The Big Lebowski*. These verbs transform iconic quotes and memorable moments into present participles that read naturally when displayed with duration (e.g., "Abiding for 30s").
