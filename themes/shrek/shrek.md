# Shrek Spinner Verbs

> "Ogres are like onions... they have layers!"

A swamp-tastic collection of spinner verbs inspired by the entire Shrek franchise. From the original fairy tale parody to Far Far Away adventures, these verbs capture the humor, heart, and iconic moments that made Shrek a cultural phenomenon.
