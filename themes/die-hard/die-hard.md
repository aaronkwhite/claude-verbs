# Die Hard Theme

> "Yippee-ki-yay, motherfucker." - John McClane

Transform your Claude Code spinner into a one-man army experience with 40 verbs inspired by the Die Hard franchise. From barefoot battles in Nakatomi Plaza to explosive airport showdowns, these verbs capture the spirit of the greatest action movie ever made (don't @ us, it's also a Christmas movie).
